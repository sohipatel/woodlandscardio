# Cutting Edge Cardiac Care in Montgomery County

Woodlands Cardio was established by Dr. Sanjay Patel in 2010 to serve the residents of Montgomery County. Woodlands Cardio is committed to providing patient care that is compassionate, appropriate and effective for the treatment of cardiovascular and peripheral vascular disease and the promotion of health.

Our patient care philosophy is founded on the principle of working in partnership with patients and their families. Our patient care strategy includes prevention, early detection, comprehensive evaluation of treatment options, and a multidisciplinary approach to therapy.
